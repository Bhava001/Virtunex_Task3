Food is essential for human survival. It provides energy and nutrients to grow, move, and function. Food helps build strong muscles, bones, and teeth. Food helps keep the body warm and repair injuries. Food helps the immune system prevent or fight disease.

Different types of Food

Food can be raw, processed, or formulated. Food includes fruits, vegetables, grains, meats, dairy, and seafood. Different countries have their own specialty of dishes.



Healthy diet

A healthy diet includes a variety of foods and minimizes junk food. Fruits, vegetables, and grains in their natural form are healthier. Fried and sugary foods are not considered healthy. Eating a variety of foods and consuming less salt, sugars, and saturated fats are essential for a healthy diet. A healthy diet protects against many chronic noncommunicable diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes, and cancer.
